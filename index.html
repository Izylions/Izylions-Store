FROM BANGSAR TO THE WORLD

IZYLIONS was founded in 2013 by a group of passionate Malaysian athletes who couldn't find performance wear that truly fit the Southeast Asian body, climate, and spirit. We started with a small factory in Shah Alam and a dream to make world-class athletic wear accessible to everyone.

Today, IZYLIONS is Malaysia's leading homegrown athletic brand, worn by professional footballers, weekend warriors, yoga enthusiasts, and everyone in between. Our collections are designed with deep respect for our tropical climate — breathable, moisture-wicking, and built to withstand the heat.

OUR MISSION

To empower every Malaysian to move more, feel better, and look incredible doing it — without breaking the bank. We believe performance wear shouldn't be a luxury. It should be for everyone.

SUSTAINABILITY

By 2026, 60% of our fabrics will be made from recycled materials. We partner with local suppliers, reduce our carbon footprint with sea freight where possible, and our packaging is 100% recyclable. Because the planet matters too.
